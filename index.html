TikTok Research Project

This website is the official public information page for a TikTok developer application operated for academic research by A. Knueppel at Anglia Ruskin University, Cambridge.

The purpose of this page is to provide transparency regarding the project, its use of the TikTok developer platform, and the policies governing the collection and use of any data accessed through the TikTok API.

About the Project

This research project studies TikTok content and platform dynamics as part of ongoing academic research at Anglia Ruskin University. The project uses publicly available TikTok content to better understand social media trends and consumer behaviour.

The research may involve the analysis of publicly accessible content and metadata provided through TikTok's official developer tools in accordance with TikTok’s developer policies and terms.

App Purpose

The associated TikTok developer application is used exclusively for academic research purposes. It is not a commercial service and is not intended to provide consumer-facing features.

The application may access TikTok data through authorized APIs where such access is permitted by TikTok’s developer platform and platform policies.

How TikTok Data Is Used

Any TikTok data accessed through the developer platform is used solely for legitimate academic research purposes.

  • Used exclusively for academic research
  • No commercial use of collected data
  • No sale or redistribution of personal data
  • Use limited to research analysis and reporting
  • Access only through TikTok-approved developer tools

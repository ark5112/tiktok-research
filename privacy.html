Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This application supports an academic research project examining gender representation in TikTok videos and the potential impact of gendered content on cognitive performance in women.

Data Collection

The application may access publicly available TikTok content through official developer tools and the TikTok Research API. Information accessed may include publicly available video metadata such as captions, hashtags, engagement metrics, and publication time.

No private TikTok account information, login credentials, or personal messages are collected by this application.

Purpose of Data Use

Any data accessed through this application is used solely for academic research and educational purposes. The goal of the project is to better understand gender representation and exposure to gendered content on social media platforms.

Data Sharing

Data collected through this research will not be sold, distributed, or used for commercial purposes. Research findings may be published in academic papers or presentations but will be reported in aggregate form and will not identify individual TikTok users.

Data Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are taken to store research data securely and limit access to authorized researchers involved in the project.

Third-Party Services

This research project interacts with publicly available content on the TikTok platform. TikTok’s own handling of user data is governed by its separate privacy policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this research project or the handling of data, please contact the researcher responsible for this study.

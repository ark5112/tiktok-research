Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the TikTok Research Project operated by A. Knueppel at Anglia Ruskin University, Cambridge handles information in connection with its TikTok developer application.

Project Operator

A. Knueppel
Anglia Ruskin University, Cambridge
Email: ark138@pgr.aru.ac.uk

Information Accessed

The project may access publicly available TikTok data through TikTok’s official developer platform and APIs where such access is permitted.

Purpose of Data Use

Any data accessed through TikTok’s developer platform is used exclusively for academic research purposes, including the analysis of social media trends, platform dynamics, and related research questions.

Data Sharing

The project does not sell personal data or distribute data for commercial purposes. Data may only be used within the scope of academic research and institutional oversight requirements.

Data Retention

Data is retained only for as long as necessary to complete the research objectives or to comply with institutional research policies.

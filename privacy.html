Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This application supports an academic research project examining gender representation in TikTok videos and the impact of gendered content on cognitive performance in women.

Data Collection

The application may access publicly available TikTok content through official developer tools and the TikTok Research API. Information accessed may include captions, hashtags, engagement metrics, and publication time.

No private TikTok account information, login credentials, or personal messages are collected.

Purpose of Data Use

Data is used solely for academic and educational research purposes.

Data Sharing

Data collected in this research will not be sold or used for commercial purposes. Research results may be published in aggregated form without identifying individual TikTok users.

Contact

Questions about this research project can be directed to the project researcher.

Privacy Policy Last updated: March 2026 This application supports an academic research project examining gender representation in TikTok videos and the potential impact of gendered content on cognitive performance in women. Data Collection The application may access publicly available TikTok content through the official TikTok Research API and developer tools. The information accessed may include publicly available video metadata such as captions, hashtags, engagement metrics, and publication time. No private TikTok account information, login credentials, or personal messages are collected through this application. Purpose of Data Use Any data accessed through this application is used solely for academic research and educational purposes. The research aims to better understand patterns of gender representation and content exposure on social media platforms. Data Use and Sharing Data collected through the application will not be sold, distributed, or used for commercial purposes. Research findings may be published in academic articles, presentations, or reports, but results will be presented in aggregate form and will not identify individual TikTok users. Data Security Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to store research data securely and limit access to authorized researchers involved in the project. Data Retention Data will be retained only for as long as necessary to complete the research project and comply with applicable research and institutional policies. Third-Party Services This project interacts with publicly available content on the TikTok platform through official developer tools. The handling of data by TikTok itself is governed by TikTok’s own privacy policy. Contact If you have questions about this research project or the handling of data, please contact the project researcher responsible for this study.
